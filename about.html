About Me

I have spent a number of years working in various jobs and in between this I have dabbled in designing various items for friends (A website, a blog, an album cover or two, and the odd ticket/flyer).

Not really being satisfied with what I had done before I searched for something with prospects that would combine my interest in all things computer related and stumbled upon Codeacademy and Robert Stowe's guide to getting into web development.

I hope after clocking up a large number of hours with various books, and websites that I might be able to carve out a career for myself.

 

As an aspiring Front-end Developer, Andrew applies his enthusiasm for great design and his readiness to learn new skills to improve his level of expertise.
